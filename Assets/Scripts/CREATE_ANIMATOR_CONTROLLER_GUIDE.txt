2. Look at Animator component
3. Check the "Avatar" field

   c. In Inspector, switch to "Rig" tab
   d. Set Animation Type: Humanoid
   e. Click "Apply"
   f. An Avatar will be created automatically
   g. Drag it to the Animator's Avatar field
